This is an entry for the CoolMath Game Jam. Game Design Document written by Poof. 


Themes: Financial Literacy 
Genre: Strategy / Resource Management / Simulation Card Game 

Premise of the Game: 
Balance the economy through strategic financial policies in a choice-driven card game. Each decision shapes market stability, national debt, and economic growth as you experiment with different economic policy approaches every playthrough. Manage competing interests, respond to shifting financial pressures, and guide your kingdom toward prosperity or economic collapse.

Lessons Students will Learn When Playing: 

Problem Solving - Elements of resource management and selection of proper financial policies will provide the player with opportunities to feel confident their intelligence
Creativity - Players craft their own economic identity by choosing which policies, industries, and financial priorities define their nation. Each combination of decisions creates a different style of economy, allowing players to experiment with unique approaches.
Econmic Sustainability - Players craft their own economic identity by choosing which policies, industries, and financial priorities define their nation. Each combination of decisions creates a different style of economy, allowing players to experiment with unique approaches.

1. The core gameplay loop consists of:
2. Reviewing the current state of the kingdom
3. Receiving a policy/event card
4. Choosing one of two policy responses
5. Watching resources and the kingdom visually change
Resources are represented through simple readable meters and environmental changes within the kingdom. These values determine whether the kingdom prospers, stagnates, or collapses.
